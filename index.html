About Me

I am a variety speedrunner since 2016. My first ever speedgame was Resident Evil 4 on the ps2. In 2018~ I also started running
Devil May Cry 3 and 4. DMC4 unfortunately had no autosplitter back then, that is the reason why I started coding. Thanks to my
laziness of splitting the timer by myself I had the goal to create an autosplitter for this game. I only managed to create a portion
of the splitter from what it is today. I left the project open with the mindset to finish it one day. August 2019 I took coding as my
primary class in high school, all because of the dmc4 splitter that had caught my interest. During the pandemic in July 2020 I picked up
the project again, with more knowledge this time. I managed to finish the dmc4 splitter and was asked to also improve the dmc3 splitter
then the dmc1 and dmc2 splitter and so on. (Documentation under Autosplitter) Since then I was recommended from runner to runner to
create several splitters and since July 2021 also SRTs. Coding has become a big part of my life and I am looking forward to learn new
things everyday and improve my knowledge even further.
